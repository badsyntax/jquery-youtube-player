jQuery Youtube Player

The jQuery Youtube Player plugin builds a custom youtube video player, toolbar and playlist area.

Examples:

  • Basic
  • Advanced
  • Multiple Playlists
  • Custom Playlist
  • Positioning
  • Custom toolbar buttons
  • Themes
